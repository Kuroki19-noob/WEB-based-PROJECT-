Adobo


Our Pork and Chicken Adobo is a symphony of textures and deep, developed flavors that have been simmering to perfection. We take the lean, succulent protein of chicken and pair it with the rich, melt-in-your-mouth tenderness of premium pork belly to create the ultimate Filipino comfort experience.


"The Best of the Best"

lechon kawali


If you’re looking for the ultimate indulgence, look no further. Lechon Kawali is the gold standard of Filipino comfort food expertly seasoned pork belly that is boiled until tender, air-dried for the perfect cure, and then deep-fried until it reaches a level of shatteringly crisp perfection.


"It was called Jewel of The Diner"

Menudo


In Filipino culture, Menudo is the vibrant centerpiece of every celebration. It is a rich, colorful tomato-based stew that balances savory protein with fresh, diced vegetables. Unlike other stews, Menudo is famous for its uniform, bite-sized cuts, ensuring that every spoonful delivers a perfect medley of flavors and textures.


"The Classic"

dinuguan


Dinuguan is a masterpiece of Filipino gastronomy. Often called "chocolate meat" for its deep, rich color and silky texture, this dish is a soulful, savory stew that offers a flavor profile unlike anything else in the world.


"Chocolate Meat that melts to your mouth"

sinigang


If there is one dish that defines the Filipino palate, it is Sinigang. Known for its soul-warming, sour, and savory broth, it is the ultimate "comfort in a bowl." It’s the dish we turn to on rainy days, for family lunches, or whenever you need a revitalizing burst of flavor.


"Comfort Food of anyone"

sisig


Get ready for the dish that Anthony Bourdain famously called "the gateway to Filipino food." Sisig is an absolute masterpiece of texture and flavor—a smoky, savory, and tangy delight that sizzles as it hits your table.

Originating from the culinary capital of the Philippines, our Sisig is a tribute to tradition, meticulously prepared to give you that iconic crunch and rich, umami depth.


"Sizzling Sensation"

Taho


If you’re looking for the ultimate Filipino comfort in a cup, look no further than Taho. This beloved street food classic is the perfect way to start your morning or treat yourself to a sweet afternoon pick-me-up. It’s a simple yet heavenly combination of textures that has been a staple of Filipino childhoods for generations.


"Silky Smooth Hot Drink"
