Lagman’s Diner

Lagman’s Father Legacy

Lagman’s Diner was born from the vision of Lolo Domingo, an old man who wanted to recreate the warmth of his childhood home through the spirit of Salo-Salo. Designed to feel like a true Filipino household, the diner features Capiz windows and long wooden tables where strangers are encouraged to share a meal like family.
